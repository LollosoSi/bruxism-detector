Daytime Training

The Bruxism Detector uses the principle of classical conditioning to teach you to relax your jaw during sleep.

During the day, the app will occasionally play a beep sound at random times.
Relax your jaw as soon as you hear it.
Over time, this builds an automatic habit: the beep becomes associated with jaw relaxation.

At night, the same beep will be used when the device detects a clenching event.
Ideally, your body will respond by relaxing automatically, thanks to your daytime training.
If clenching continues despite the beep, the alarm is triggered in an attempt to wake you up.

You can stop the alarm from any ringing device in two ways:

  • Press the power button on your phone (if tracking with Android)
  • Or press the physical button on the Arduino device
